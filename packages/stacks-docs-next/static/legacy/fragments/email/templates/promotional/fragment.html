A starter template for announcements, updates, and promotions. This template is meant to be adapted and built upon. If you’re working on a major announcement, consider designing something unique.

Good For: Not So Good For:
Announcements that are worthy of grabbing attention or celebrating, such as: Emails that a user may receive multiple times or emails with a negative connotation, such as:
New product feature New answers
Welcome to Teams Job recommendations
Welcome to Talent Out-of-region applicants
You’ve reached 10k reputation Your admin privileges have been revoked

Note: These templates are in Iterable and Hubspot. The code here can also be copied and pasted.

Emails in our codebase use a main template (eg. _Stacks-Layout.Html.cshtml) that includes the <head>, logo, and footer. Only the middle part of these templates are needed (between <!-- EMAIL BODY : BEGIN --> and <!-- EMAIL BODY : END --> code comments).
