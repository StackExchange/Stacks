Our vision is the reason we exist. It’s the beyond-our-wildest dream ambition that we will always strive to achieve. ## Brand Positioning

Brand positionings are strategic in nature, and for an internal managerial audience rather than an external consumer audience. They help guide the tactical execution of the brand and are often the starting point for developing marketing messages for customers and consumers.

### Stack Overflow is a.

**Technology hub**

It’s a central place for technologists; a pivotal, flexible and people-focused resource at the heart of the tech industry.

### That.

**Unlocks growth and shapes progress**

Stack Overflow powers personal development, career advancement, business success and societal impact. For everyone from beginners to experienced professionals, Stack Overflow unlocks growth and shapes progress.

### In a way that is.

**Useful, Community-powered and Authoritative**

The key associations come together to create a picture of Stack Overflow that feels insightful, trustworthy and human in the face of a world that’s turning to AI. They build on the brand’s equity and create an opportunity for it to own its leadership position.

## Brand Story

Founded in 2008, Stack Overflow’s original mission was to democratize access to verified technical knowledge. The world, and the internet with it, has transformed dramatically since then, particularly with the rise of AI and Large Language Model companies. This evolution has compelled us to adapt.

For years, the Stack Overflow community’s commitment to quality, achieved through very strict moderation, built a vast and accurate Q&A knowledge base. While this was a boon for ensuring high-quality content, this same strictness over time also contributed to a decline in contributions. More recently, the emergence of powerful AI technologies—often trained on our meticulously curated data—has further shifted the landscape. These AI systems offer user-friendly interfaces for accessing technical knowledge, challenging the traditional relevance of our site.

These profound changes prompted us to fundamentally reevaluate how Stack Overflow can best serve our users and customers in this new era. We’re excited to share our evolved company vision and mission.
